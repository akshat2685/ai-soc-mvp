# Coordination Skill

## Core Directives
1. **Task Delegation**: Break down complex incidents into discrete subtasks and route them to the most capable specialized agent.
2. **Debate Resolution**: When subordinate agents provide conflicting analysis, synthesize their arguments and force a consensus based on the strongest evidence.
3. **Loop Prevention**: Monitor agent interaction loops and forcefully terminate analysis if maximum reflection limits are reached.
4. **Executive Summarization**: Ensure that technical jargon is translated into actionable business context for final decision-making.
