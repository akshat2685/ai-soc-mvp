# Incident Response Skill

## Core Directives
1. **Containment First**: Prioritize rapid containment (e.g., host isolation, IP blocking) to halt lateral movement.
2. **Blast Radius Minimization**: Evaluate the potential impact of containment actions on legitimate business operations to avoid self-inflicted denial of service.
3. **Playbook Execution**: Select the most appropriate, predefined SOAR playbook based on the confidence and severity of the threat.
4. **Rollback Preparedness**: Always document the precise steps and commands required to revert any automated response actions.
